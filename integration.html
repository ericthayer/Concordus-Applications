Integration Services

Concordus Applications is a Systems Integrator that is focused on building a connected world by offering innovative, smart and cost-effective solutions. Integration is our core strength, it is what we have done for a long time, it our bread and butter!

We deliver value through integration. We are brilliant in Integration because we understand the business objectives our Customers want us to deliver through Integration. We offer Integration solutions for data, infrastructure and applications. We do everything possible around Integration - Strategy Consulting, Architecture, design, development, testing, deployment, support and maintenance.

We have a strong team of experienced integrators that are experts in API development and API deployment on different infrastructure, Service Oriented Architectures (SOA), Microservices Architectures (MSA), Cloud platform based SaaS (Software as a Service), IaaS (Infrastructure as a Service) and PaaS (Platform as a Service).

The Concordus team has deep understanding and capability in designingsolutions ground up to take advantage of cloud's extreme elasticity and physical availability across multiple geographies. Our experience covers a vast range of industries like Communications, Channel, Retail, Health Care, Government entities like Income Tax and Immigration, Financial Services, Banking, Equity Markets etc.

We follow an API-led connectivity approach in the integration of apps, data, and devices anywhere, on premise or in the cloud. In addition to API Development and hosting, we have extensive experience with Identity solutions (IDaaS or Identity as a Service), Single Sign-on (SSO), Cloud Access Security Broker (CASB), Data Loss Prevention (DLP) etc. We understand how to build end-to-end business workflows using workflow management software.

We follow Integration industry best practices to connect disparate on premise and cloud apps such as CRMs, ERPs, Payments, Office 365, Gmail, Slack, Email Marketing etc.
